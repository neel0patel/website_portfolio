Recipe Application

I collaborated with a team of five individuals to develop a recipe application focused on storing recipes in an online database. To streamline our workflow, we divided the group into front-end (FE) and back-end (BE) development teams. Our primary objective for this application was to enable users to effortlessly add, save, edit, and delete recipes, providing a seamless recipe management experience.

Task Management Tool

This project was a result of my individual effort, where I focused on authenticity, functionality, and robust data management to deliver an exceptional user experience. By recreating the "Things" application for my final project with NJIT, I incorporated JS, React, and Shell in both the front-end and back-end development. This comprehensive approach allowed for seamless creation, editing, and deletion of daily tasks. Leveraging mongoDB, I efficiently stored tasks and user data while ensuring secure collection and encryption with "passport.js." By integrating "jwt," I enhanced security by generating hashed passwords during user registration. Overall, my project represents a culmination of my skills and dedication, aiming to provide an optimal user experience.
